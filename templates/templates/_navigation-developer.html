Platforms

Ubuntu is the workstation OS of choice for developers and devops, enabling frictionless deployment from dev to test and production, on server, and connected devices.

Build devices and apps for the Internet of Things with robust security, app stores and reliable updates. Ubuntu Core is a tiny, transactional edition of Ubuntu that supports snap packages and powers connected devices from gateways and switches to self-driving cars, drones, robots and industrial systems.

Ubuntu is the leading cloud guest OS, running most workloads in public clouds today, with a particular strength in containers and machine learning.

Tools

Package, distribute, and update any app for Linux with snapcraft. Snaps are containerised software packages that auto-update and are safe to run.

Operate your complex software stacks like Hadoop, Spark and Kubernetes, on all major clouds, bare metal and VMware with Juju, the open source model-driven operations tool.

Dynamically customize cloud instances and LXD containers with user data and cloud-init, supporting all major operating systems.

Deploy the Canonical Distribution of Kubernetes or OpenStack on a single machine or a whole cluster, with conjure-up.
